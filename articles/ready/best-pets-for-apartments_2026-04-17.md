## What to Look For

Before buying, consider these key factors for apartment living:

### 1. Easy to Clean
In a small space, messes are more disruptive. Prioritize products with washable covers, waterproof linings, or surfaces that wipe clean quickly.

### 2. Compact Size
Measure your space before buying. The best apartment pet products are thoughtfully sized — substantial enough to serve their purpose, compact enough not to dominate a room.

### 3. Odor Control
Apartments trap smells. Look for products designed with odor-resistant materials, especially for beds, food, and litter.

### 4. Noise Level
Thin walls mean your neighbors will hear everything. Avoid squeaky toys late at night and invest in products that minimize disruption.

### 5. Storage-Friendly
If a product can't be stored or folded when not in use, it will permanently occupy your limited floor space. Lightweight and stackable wins every time.
